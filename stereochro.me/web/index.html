stereochrome

Welcome!

stereochro.me was the online notebook of me, Keith Gaughan. It’s here that I used to post up anything I write that others may find interesting, or useful, or neither. It’s fair to say that what you’ll find here is an ad-hoc mess of half-baked ideas, barely functional software, random thoughts, and the odd semblance of a clue. I’ve since moved the content to more suitable locations and added redirects.

I keep a weblog here, but after I rewrote the code powering this site, it became more of a tumblelog. Whenever I find something interesting, or post something notable here, I post a link to it there. I also use it for short notes.
