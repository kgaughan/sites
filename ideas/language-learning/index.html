Language learning

Earlier this summer, I started brushing up on my French, and learning Spanish and Portuguese using Duolingo. More recently, I started learning some Dutch too for when I attend FOSDEM. Here are some resources I’m using to help me.

General

  • Forvo - for getting the proper pronunciation of words.
  • Linguee - good for figuring out the context in which words are used.
  • Reverso dictionary.
  • Tatoeba - a database of example sentences translated into different languages.
  • Verbix - verb conjugations.
  • Study aids.
  • FSI language courses - dated, but free and comprehensive.

Dutch

  • DutchGrammar.com.

French

  • ebooks libres et gratuits - Free e-books in French.
  • Practice French verbs.
  • Larousse.

Portuguese

  • Conjuga-me - Portuguese verb conjugations.
  • Practice Portuguese - I’m learning Brazilian Portuguese, but I just found this, and I figure it’s a good idea to at least become familiar with European Portuguese.
